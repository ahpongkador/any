    <h3>5G vs 2.4G WiFi Signal</h3>
      <p>2.4 GHz WiFi will support up to 450 Mbps or 600 Mbps, while 5 GHz Wi-Fi will support up to 1300 Mbps.</p>
    
    <h3>How to Change WiFi Name and Password</h3>
      <ol>
      <li>Connect to your router.</li>
      <li>Open a browser and enter your router's IP.</li>
      <li>Log in with your router's username and password.</li>
      <li>Find Wireless Settings.</li>
      <li>Change the WiFi name (SSID).</li>
      <li>Change the password.</li>
      <li>Save your changes.</li>
      <li>Reconnect your devices to the new WiFi network.</li>
    </ol>
